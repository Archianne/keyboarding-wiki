# Privacy Policy for Keyboarding

**Effective Date:** March 19, 2026

## Introduction
This privacy policy outlines the data processing practices for the "Keyboarding" Android application.

## Data Collection and Privacy
"Keyboarding" is an offline Android keyboard application that utilizes on-device AI. 
We take your privacy extremely seriously.

1. **Zero Data Collection:** The Keyboard **does not** collect, log, transmit, or share any personal information.
2. **No Cloud Connectivity:** All keystrokes, text inputs, artificial intelligence inference, and interactions occur entirely locally on your device. We do not have servers that receive your typing data.
3. **Android Permissions:** The application requires the standard Android Input Method Editor (IME) binding permission (`android.permission.BIND_INPUT_METHOD`) to function as a keyboard. This permission does not grant internet access or the ability to exfiltrate data.
4. **Third-Party Services:** We do not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs.

## Children's Privacy
Our application does not knowingly collect or solicit any information from anyone under the age of 13.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. 

## Contact Us
If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact the developer.
